Meet Dr. Laura Marques

Featured Photo Placeholder

Dr. Laura Marques is a psychologist and a mother of two elementary students in Leander ISD. Prior to her appointment to the Leander ISD Board of Trustees in November 2025, she served on the PTA, site-based committees, and as the Advocacy Chair of the Leander ISD Special Programs Parent Advisory Council Executive Board.

Dr. Marques has spent her career working with kids, families, and educators by supporting students who needed extra help, partnering with schools to build systems that work for every learner, and helping train professionals who serve children and communities. Laura has seen what works, what doesn't, and what happens when kids don't get the support they need early enough. She also understands that supporting educators is essential for success.

Laura knows what it means to be a family that needs something more from their school. That personal experience drives her commitment to empowering educators and parents and making sure every child has someone in their corner.

Laura is running for the Leander ISD Board of Trustees because she believes deeply in what public education can be, and she is committed to doing the hard, thoughtful work to get it there for every single child.
