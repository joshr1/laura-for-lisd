Priorities for LISD

Every child in Leander ISD deserves a high-quality education and the support they need to thrive. Here's what Laura fights for as a Trustee.

🎓

Educational Excellence for ALL Students

Every child in Leander ISD deserves a high-quality education, not just the ones whose path is straightforward. That means high standards that build creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills, alongside strong support for students who need something different.

I advocate for vital programs that serve diverse learners and for the board's goal of closing the gaps, because excellence means excellence for ALL.

That vision depends on great teachers. I listen, amplify educator voices, advocate for reducing unnecessary burden, and support keeping great educators in our classrooms.

Educational Excellence Teacher Support
💡

Fiscal Responsibility & Smart Innovation

Leander ISD is navigating a wave of state legislation that chips away at local decision-making and comes with underfunding and unfunded mandates that strain our budget. As a trustee, I will continue advocating to the state for adequate and equitable funding and for the right of our community to make its own decisions.

In the face of the serious funding crisis for school districts across Texas, Leander ISD must be both fiscally responsible and innovative. I will continue advocating for eliminating inefficiencies with little impact on student experience, transitioning to operational models that reflect LISD's size as a large district, implementing proven systems like PLCs and MTSS that improve outcomes and reduce costly interventions, and using innovative approaches that reimagine education delivery in ways that save money and improve results.

Leander ISD's challenges require smart solutions. As a trustee, I advocate for strategic planning, innovative partnerships, and responsible stewardship of taxpayer resources while prioritizing student opportunities and staff support.

Fiscal Responsibility Local Control Innovation
🤝

Community Partnerships

Public schools are at the heart of thriving communities, and supportive communities are essential to strong schools. I believe in listening, collaborating, and building trust through open communication and meaningful community engagement.

Community partnerships with local businesses and community organizations are essential to fill resource gaps, create real-world learning opportunities, and prepare students for a rapidly changing workforce. Strong public schools make our community a better place for everyone, including those who don't have children in the system.

As a trustee, I advocate for meaningful stakeholder input, clear communication about district decisions, and partnerships with local businesses and community organizations to fill resource gaps and create real-world learning opportunities.

Community Transparency Partnerships
🤖

Responsible Technology & AI in the Classroom

Our students must be prepared to be successful in a changing environment driven by technology and AI. As a trustee, I will continue working with the board, administration, and community to support policies that equip students with relevant tools while protecting healthy development, critical thinking, creativity, and human connection.
