About Laura

Meet Dr. Laura Marques

Dr. Laura Marques is a psychologist, a mother of two elementary students in Leander ISD, and a board-appointed Trustee. She has spent her career working with kids, families, and educators — supporting students who needed extra help and building systems that work for every learner.

Laura is running for the Leander ISD Board of Trustees because she believes deeply in what public education can be, and she is committed to doing the hard, thoughtful work to get it there for every single child.
