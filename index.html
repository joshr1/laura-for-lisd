About Laura

Meet Dr. Laura Marques

I am a mother of two Leander ISD elementary students, a psychologist, and a Leander ISD Trustee. I have spent my career working with kids, families, and educators, supporting students who needed something different and building systems that work for every learner.

I am running to continue serving on the Leander ISD Board of Trustees because I believe deeply in what public education can be, and am committed to doing the hard, thoughtful work to get it there for every single child.
